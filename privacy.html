Privacy Policy — Liars Grid

Effective date: 28 June 2026 · Contact: katinkia@gmail.com

Who we are

Liars Grid (“the app”) is an independent iOS puzzle app published by Coralia Digital. “We” means the individual developer publishing the app through the Apple App Store.

Do I need an account?

No. Liars Grid is fully playable without signing in, and your progress is kept on your device. Creating an account is optional and only adds syncing of your stats and streak across your devices.

What data we collect and why

Account data

If you choose to create an account, you provide an email address and a password. We use your email address to create and identify your account and to let you sign in. Authentication and password storage are handled securely by our backend provider, Supabase; we never see or store your password in readable form. We do not use your email for marketing.

Puzzle activity

When you are signed in, we store your gameplay statistics so we can sync them across your devices: number of puzzles solved, number of clean (hint-free) solves, your best level, your current and best streaks, your daily-challenge streaks, the dates you have played, and an optional display name. This data is linked to your account.

Data stored only on your device

Your in-progress puzzle state and your app settings are stored only on your device using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults). If you never sign in, all of your progress stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

What we do NOT collect

Location data, contacts, photos or camera, microphone, health or fitness data, browsing or search history, and device identifiers for advertising (IDFA). The app contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites. Liars Grid has no in-app purchases or subscriptions, so we never receive any payment information.

Where data is stored

If you create an account, your email and gameplay statistics are stored using Supabase, hosted in the United States. By creating an account you consent to this transfer.

Data retention

Your account data is stored for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (Settings → Account → Delete Account inside the app), all of your associated data is permanently deleted from our servers. This cannot be undone.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access the data we hold about you, correct inaccurate data, delete your data (use Settings → Account → Delete Account, or email us), or request a copy of your data. To exercise any of these rights, email katinkia@gmail.com.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after the date of the change constitutes acceptance.
