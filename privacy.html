Privacy Policy — Liars Grid

Effective date: 14 June 2026 · Contact: katinkia@gmail.com

Who we are

Liars Grid (“the app”) is an indie iOS puzzle app. “We” means the individual developer publishing the app through the Apple App Store.

What data we collect and why

Account data

When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. We use this only to create and identify your account. We do not use your Apple ID for marketing.

Puzzle activity

Each time you complete a daily puzzle, we record the puzzle date (your local calendar date on your device), how long it took you to solve, how many hints you used, and the grid size. We use this to calculate and display your streak and to populate the puzzle archive. This data is linked to your account.

Subscription data

When you purchase Liars Grid Pro, Apple and RevenueCat process the transaction. We receive confirmation of your subscription status (active or not). We do not receive your payment card details.

Local-only data

Your in-progress puzzle state (which cells you’ve tapped and how long you’ve been playing) is stored only on your device using SwiftData. This data is never sent to our servers.

What we do NOT collect

Location data, contacts, photos or camera, microphone, health or fitness data, browsing history, device identifiers for advertising (IDFA), or any data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Where data is stored

Your account and puzzle data is stored in Supabase, hosted in the United States (AWS us-east-1). By using the app you consent to this transfer.

Data retention

Your data is stored for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), all your data is permanently deleted from our servers within seconds. This cannot be undone.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access the data we hold about you, correct inaccurate data, delete your data (use Settings → Delete Account, or email us), or export your data (email us). To exercise any of these rights, email katinkia@gmail.com.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after the date of the change constitutes acceptance.
