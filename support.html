How to play

Liars Grid is a daily logic-deduction puzzle. Each room in the grid contains a number telling you how many cells to paint — but one number in each room is lying. Work out which clue is false and solve the grid. There's a built-in walkthrough and hints if you get stuck: tap the “?” on any puzzle for a refresher.

Common questions

My streak didn't update. Streaks advance once per calendar day when you finish the daily set. If a day looks missing, make sure your device date is correct and that you completed all three daily grids.

I'm playing on more than one device. Sign in (Settings → Account) on each device to sync your stats and streak. Your progress is tied to your account, not the device.

I want to start fresh / delete my data. You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account inside the app. This is permanent and can't be undone.

Do I need an account? No — Liars Grid is fully playable without signing in. An account only adds cross-device sync of your streak and archive.
